Thom Yorke

7 October 1968

Lead Vocals, guitar, piano, keyboard, songwriter Thomas Edward "Thom" Yorke - a multi-instrumentalist, Yorke mainly plays guitar and piano, but also instruments including keyboards, bass, and drums, and works extensively with synthesisers, sequencers and programming. He is known for his falsetto vocals; in 2008, Rolling Stone ranked him the 66th greatest singer of all time.

Ed O'Brien

15 April 1968

Guitar, percussion, backing vocals Edward John "Ed" O'Brien is an English guitarist best known as a member of the alternative rock band Radiohead, with whom he has recorded nine studio albums. He makes extensive use of effects units to create atmospheric sounds and textures. In 2015, Rolling Stone named O'Brien the 59th greatest guitarist of all time.

Jonny Greenwood

5 November 1971

Lead guitar, keyboards, ondes Martenot, analogue synthesisers, other instruments Jonathan Richard Guy "Jonny" Greenwood - a multi-instrumentalist, Greenwood also plays instruments including the bass guitar, piano, viola, and drums, and is a prominent player of the ondes Martenot, an early electronic instrument. He works with electronic techniques such as programming, sampling and looping, and writes music software used by Radiohead. He has been named one of the greatest guitarists of all time by publications including the NME, Rolling Stone and Spin.

Colin Greenwood

26 June 1969

Bass guitar, keyboards Colin Charles Greenwood is an English musician best known as the bassist for the alternative rock band Radiohead. He also plays keyboards and synthesisers and works on sampling on the electronic side of Radiohead. He is the older brother of lead Radiohead guitarist Jonny Greenwood.

Philip Selway

23 May 1967

Drums, percussion, backing vocals Philip James "Phil" Selway is an English musician, singer, and songwriter best known as the drummer of English rock group Radiohead. In addition to drums, he provides backing vocals, along with occasional guitar and lead vocals, for 7 Worlds Collide. Selway is well known for his precision and proficiency in various styles and unusual time signatures, being named the 26th greatest drummer of all time by Gigwise in 2008.
